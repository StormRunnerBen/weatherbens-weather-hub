Privacy Policy
WeatherBen's Weather Hub — weatherbenwxhub.com
Last updated: June 2026
The Short Version
We do not collect, store, sell, or share your personal information. WeatherBen's Weather Hub is a weather tool, not a data business. Your location is used only to fetch weather for you. Your preferences stay on your device. We do not run ads. We do not have user accounts.
What Data Is Involved

WeatherBen's Weather Hub does not have a backend server or database. No personal information is transmitted to or stored by us. Here is a complete picture of what data flows where when you use the app:

Data What happens to it Stored by us?
GPS coordinates Sent to Open-Meteo (weather) and BigDataCloud (reverse geocoding) to look up your local forecast. Never stored by us. No
City searches Sent to Open-Meteo's geocoding API to find matching cities. Never stored by us. No
Saved locations & theme preference Stored only in your browser's localStorage on your own device. Never transmitted anywhere. No — your device only
Feedback form submissions If you submit the feedback form, your message and any details you provide are sent to Netlify Forms. This is the only place user-submitted data touches a third-party server. By Netlify only
Your Browser Storage

The app uses your browser's localStorage to remember your saved locations, last-searched city, theme preference, and which version of What's New you've seen. This data:

— Lives only on your device and is never transmitted to any server
— Is not a cookie and does not require consent under most privacy frameworks
— Can be cleared at any time through your browser's settings (Settings → Privacy → Clear browsing data, or equivalent)

To clear all app data: In your browser settings, clear site data for weatherbenwxhub.com. This resets saved locations, theme, and all preferences to default.
Third-Party Services

The app fetches weather and map data from several public sources. When you use the app, your IP address and request details may be visible to these services under their own privacy policies:

Service What it receives Privacy policy
Open-Meteo Coordinates or city name for weather lookup open-meteo.com/en/terms
NWS / NOAA Coordinates or zone ID for alerts and radar noaa.gov/protecting-your-privacy
BigDataCloud GPS coordinates for reverse geocoding (city name lookup) bigdatacloud.com/privacy-policy
Netlify Hosts the app and receives feedback form submissions netlify.com/privacy
Google Fonts Serves the IBM Plex font family used for display policies.google.com/privacy

Open-Meteo is designed with privacy in mind and does not store personal data. All NWS and NOAA services are operated by the US federal government under their privacy framework.

Advertising

WeatherBen's Weather Hub does not display advertisements and does not work with any advertising networks. No tracking pixels, ad scripts, or behavioral targeting of any kind are present in the app.

California Residents (CCPA)

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act, California residents have the right to know what personal information is collected about them and to request that it be deleted.

WeatherBen's Weather Hub does not collect, sell, or share personal information as defined by the CCPA. We have no personal information to disclose or delete. If you submitted feedback through our feedback form, that data is held by Netlify — you can contact us at the address below to request its removal.

European Users (GDPR)

WeatherBen's Weather Hub is primarily designed for US users and US weather data coverage. If you are accessing the app from the European Union or European Economic Area, the following applies:

Legal basis for processing: When you use the "Use My Location" feature, your GPS coordinates are processed on the basis of your explicit consent (you choose to tap the button and grant location permission). Coordinates are used solely to fetch local weather and are not retained by us.

Data minimization: The app is designed to use the minimum data necessary. Location is never stored server-side. Preferences are stored locally on your device only.

Your rights: Under GDPR you have the right to access, rectify, or erase personal data held about you. Since we hold no personal data server-side, there is nothing to access or erase on our end. For data held by third-party services (Netlify, Open-Meteo, etc.), please refer to their respective privacy policies linked above.

Note: Full GDPR compliance tooling (consent management, Data Processing Agreements with all processors, formal records of processing activities) will be implemented if and when the app expands to intentionally serve EU users at scale.
Children's Privacy

WeatherBen's Weather Hub does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. The app does not have user accounts or registration of any kind.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes in a meaningful way, the "Last updated" date at the top of this page will be updated. Since the app collects no personal information, changes to this policy are unlikely to affect your privacy in practice.

Contact

Questions about this privacy policy or the app's data practices? Use the feedback form and select "Other" as the topic, or reach out directly through the app.

WeatherBen's Weather Hub is not an official weather source and is not affiliated with NWS, NOAA, or any government agency.
